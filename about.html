My Computer Background

I studied a lot of upper level computer science concepts in college. Computer vision, data mining, operating systems, and programming language grammarss are all upper level courses that I took. My education taught me *why* many things in the computer world work. I did not spend much time learning the syntax of many different programming languages, so I am grateful that TCS is giving me the opportunity to learn so much before I join their workforce properly.

I am most comfortable with Java, C, and Scala. I spent much time this summer learning C++, but there is still much more to learn, I'm sure. I took a course on Javascript this summer, as well, but all of the learning through SimpliLearn has definitely built upon what I learned myself.
