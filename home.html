Dane Anderson's Life

I mostly did work on comparative religious traditions, but theological philosophy is very interesting to me. I want to spend more of my free time learning digital audio synthesis. Software like Max MSP is terribly interesting to me, and it would be a dream to pioneer that field and create new artistic opportunities for the technologically gifted.
